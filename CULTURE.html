Culture

Filipino culture is a fusion of native beliefs and practices, Spanish Catholicism, American and Asian influences, and other Asian and Western cultures. A great majority of Philippine cultures remain to do so even today. First and foremost, a Filipino's family is their priority. Therefore, you are considered as a member of the family whether you are a direct relative or a distant relative from the third or fourth generation. We Filipinos are quite devout; most of us attend services weekly, not just on Sundays. However, that doesn't mean we don't enjoy a good party now and again we love to hold celebrations and fiestas. While Filipinos embrace many positive cultural practices, maybe the two most notable are our warm hospitality and reverence for elders.
