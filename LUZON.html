Luzon

SAGADA

Sagada is a famous destination in Northern Luzon, especially among hikers and explorers,due to its amazing natural beauties and breathtaking landscapes. This location is ideal for a romance film due to its bright yet chilly weather, gorgeous natural scenery, and sea of clouds that make you question whether you're still on Earth.

BANAUE RICE TERRACES

Banaue Rice Terraces is one of the most stunning destinations in all of Luzon. The Hagdan-Hagdang Palayan is a stunning location on the island of Luzon that brings the Ifugaos and all Filipinos worldwide immense pride. This magnificent structure was added to the UNESCO World Heritage List back in 1995 for all the world to gaze at.

INTRAMUROS

Intramuros, sometimes called the "Walled City," is a historical and cultural treasure trove. The town's restored colonial-era buildings and winding, narrow alleyways will make you question if you're still in the Philippines or somewhere else entirely. Many important events took place here hundreds of years ago, making it one of the most historically significant locations on Luzon and in the Philippines.
